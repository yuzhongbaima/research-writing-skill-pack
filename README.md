# Research Writing Skill Pack

An engineering-oriented Codex skill suite for research writing, midstream diagnosis, and human-gated semi-automated research workflows.

This pack is designed for:

- transportation engineering
- energy saving and environmental engineering
- ocean engineering
- low-altitude aircraft systems

It also supports adjacent applied-technical and computing topics, but defaults to engineering evidence, validation, and writing norms.

## Included skills

- `paper-workflow-suite`
- `research-supervision`
- `literature-review`
- `research-positioning`
- `citation-audit`
- `method-design`
- `figure-table-storytelling`
- `reviewer-preflight`
- `chapter-polisher`

## What makes this pack different

- Supports nonlinear entry from half-finished ideas, draft sections, figures, manuscripts, and reviewer comments
- Treats the interaction as coauthor-style collaboration, not just prompt servicing
- Adds human gates for semi-automated research instead of assuming full autonomy is safe
- Adapts AI-paper workflows to engineering domains with stronger attention to evidence tiers, operating conditions, uncertainty, and deployment realism
